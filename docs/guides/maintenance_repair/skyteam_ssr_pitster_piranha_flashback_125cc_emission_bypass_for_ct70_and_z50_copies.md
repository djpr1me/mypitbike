# Emission Bypass

1. At this stage, refrain from filling the fuel tank beyond halfway.
2. Initiate the motorcycle using the kick start mechanism.
3. Ensure the choke, located on the left control, is disengaged (pushed to the right) once the engine is running.
4. The manufacturer has installed a plug in the fuel screw; upon its removal, you can proceed with precise adjustments.
